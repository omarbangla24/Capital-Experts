Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about solar, battery storage, EV charging, and our services.

How long does a solar panel system last?
Quality solar panel systems are designed to last 25-30 years or more. Most premium panels come with a 25-year performance warranty guaranteeing at least 80% of their original output. With proper maintenance and monitoring, your system can continue generating clean energy well beyond this period.
How much can I save with solar panels?
Savings vary depending on your system size, energy usage, and electricity tariff. Most homeowners save between 50-90% on their electricity bills. A typical residential system pays for itself within 3-6 years through energy savings and feed-in tariff credits, then continues generating free electricity for decades.
Do I need a battery with my solar system?
A battery is not essential but highly recommended. Without a battery, excess solar energy is exported to the grid at feed-in tariff rates (typically 5-12 cents per kWh). With a battery, you store that energy for use when you need it most — evenings and nights — when grid electricity costs 25-40 cents per kWh. Batteries also provide backup power during outages.
How long does installation take?
A typical residential solar installation takes 1-2 days. Commercial installations may take 3-7 days depending on system size and complexity. Battery installations usually take half a day. We handle all compliance paperwork, grid connection applications, and STC processing so you don't have to worry about a thing.
What happens on cloudy days or at night?
Solar panels still generate electricity on cloudy days, though at reduced capacity (typically 10-25% of normal output). At night, your system switches to drawing power from the grid or from your battery if you have one installed. Modern systems with smart inverters automatically manage the transition between solar, battery, and grid power.
Are there government rebates available?
Yes! The federal government offers Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs) which provide an upfront discount on your system cost. We handle all STC paperwork and apply the discount directly to your quote. Additionally, businesses may be eligible for instant asset write-off deductions. We'll advise you on all available incentives during your consultation.
Can I finance my solar system?
Absolutely. We offer several financing options including solar loans with competitive interest rates and flexible terms up to 10 years. In many cases, your energy savings can offset the loan repayments, meaning you start saving from day one with no out-of-pocket cost. Contact us to discuss the best option for your situation.
What maintenance does a solar system need?
Solar systems require minimal maintenance. Panels are self-cleaning in most cases thanks to rain. We recommend an annual visual inspection and occasional cleaning if you're in a dusty area. We offer monitoring services that track your system's performance in real time and alert you to any issues. Our maintenance packages include periodic inspections and cleaning.
Can I upgrade my existing solar system?
Yes, we specialise in system upgrades and optimisation. Whether you want to add more panels, upgrade to a more efficient inverter, add battery storage, or integrate an EV charger, we can assess your existing system and recommend the best upgrade path to maximise your energy production and savings.
Do you provide a warranty on your work?
Yes. We provide a comprehensive workmanship warranty on all installations in addition to the manufacturer warranties on all products. Our panels come with up to 25-year performance warranties, inverters typically carry 10-year warranties, and our workmanship warranty covers the installation quality. We stand behind every project we complete.
